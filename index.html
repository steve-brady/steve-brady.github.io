Services

Package Options

  • Dobson Details offers full auto detailing services with competitively priced packages.
  • All detail pricing is dependent upon the vehicle type and overall condition.
  • We use high quality, industry proven cleaning products.
  • Complimentary vehicle pickup/delivery, on-site mobile detailing and vehicle drop off are all available options for our customers.
  • We provide free estimates with flexible, appointment-based scheduling.

Interior

  • Vaccum all interior carpet surfaces, floor mats, and trunk
  • Shampoo all carpet surfaces and floor mats
  • Treat, deodorize and full carpet extraction
  • Condition leather upholstery sears, steering wheel, shift column
  • Clean all glass surfaces including windows, touch screen, sunroof
  • Application of protective cleaner to plastic, vinyl trim and all interior surfaces
  • Clean all air duct vents/HVAC
  • Steam clean and sanatize interior surfaces/door panels
  • Clean and restoration of all-weather floor mats

Exterior

  • Pressure wash all exterior surfaces
  • Hand wash using rinseless solution (2-bucket wash process)
  • Hand dry using microfiber towels
  • Clean and dress tires and wheels
  • Clean and restore all exterior plastic trim, tonneau cover
  • Clean all exterior glass, including windows and mirrors
  • Exhaust tips polish
  • Clean inner wheel well liners
  • Application of quick wax spray
  • Microfiber final buff

Add-ons

  • Deep carpet stain removal
  • Pet hair removal
  • Decontamination Treatment, rail dust and tar removal
  • Ozone machine treatment - full odor removeal/deodorizer
  • Full engine bay steam clean and dress
  • IGL Ceramic Caotings: Paint, Ecocoat Glass, Wheels, Exterior Trim
  • Clay bar and liquid wax/paint sealant treatment
  • Multi-step pain correction: removal of swirl marks, paint imperfections and surface scratches
  • Daily driver leather seat restoration
  • Steam clean wheels treatment
